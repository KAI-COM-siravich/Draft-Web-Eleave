User Guide

Find answers to common questions and learn how to navigate the E-Leave system efficiently.

To submit a new leave request, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the main dashboard.
  2. Click on the large "Create Request" button.
  3. Select your leave type (Annual, Sick, Personal, etc.).
  4. Choose your start and end dates.
  5. Add any necessary remarks or attach a medical certificate if required.
  6. Click "Submit". Your request will be sent to your Line Manager for approval.

Your remaining leave balance is always visible on your main dashboard under the "Leave Balances" section. It shows a breakdown of your entitlement, used days, and remaining days for each leave category (e.g., Annual Leave: 10/15 days used).

You can view the full list of official company holidays by clicking the "Yearly Holidays" icon in the bottom menu bar of the Home page. It will display a schedule that you can filter by year.

Navigate to the "Employees" page via the bottom dock. If you have admin permissions, you can add new employees by clicking the "Onboard Employee" card at the bottom. You can also edit or delete existing records by hovering over their card and clicking the respective icons.
